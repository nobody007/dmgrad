Assessment and Evaluation

Students need to form groups that consist of two or three students by the end of Week 5. There will be 9-10 homework and one term project. Each group need hand in the project reports and is required to give a presentation about its term project at the end of the semester. The homework accounts for 70% of the final grade and the term project accounts for 30%.
