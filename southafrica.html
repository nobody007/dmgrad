Example : South African Heart Disease

- Textbook (The elements of Statistical Learning, p100.)

 

This is an example of Logistic regression. You can download the data from here.

The aim of the study : " To establish the intensity of ischemic heart disease risk factors in that high-incidence region."

The data represent white males between 15 and 64, the response variable is the presence or absence of myocardial infarction at the time of survey.

 

First read data into table.

sfdata<-read.table("h:\\my documents\\stat598m\\southafrica.txt",header=T, sep=",")

Now fit the logistic regression model using GLM with binomial family and logit function.

res1<-glm(chd~sbp+tobacco+ldl+famhist+obesity+alcohol+age, data=sfdata,family=binomial(link="logit"))
