Dataming Final Project

The final project is data analysis or a paper presentation.

1. Data analysis

Get data from internet or other source and analyze them using methods covered in the class (it is ok even if it's not covered). You can pick any type of data. However, contact me first before you start analyzing data.

2. Paper presentation

Every group is required to read a paper, understand the theory, methodology and examples in the paper, and present it to the class. You also need to hand in a brief summary of the paper with your comments after presentation. You can pick any paper introduced in the class or the textbook. It is even o.k that you can pick a paper that is not covered in the class. It is a good idea that you send me a paper and get my approval to use it for the project.

You are strongly encouraged to implement the methods in the paper. You don't need to implement the whole algorithm but try as much as possible. Try to duplicate the results of papers (tables or plots). Try to implement it in simple condition if it is too hard for the general conditions.

The presentation is 30 minutes. There will be 5 minutes question time.
